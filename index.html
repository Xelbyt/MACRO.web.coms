What is ETP?

The California Employment Training Panel (ETP) provides funding to employers to assist in upgrading the skills of their workers through training that leads to good paying, long-term jobs. MACRO Development partners with San Bernardino Community College District (SBCCD) to bring these benefits directly to your organization.

Training costs are covered through state funding‚Äîmaking upskilling affordable and accessible for California businesses.

How Does It Work?

MACRO Development acts as both the training provider and convener to state funds. All ETP compliance, documentation, and reporting is managed by SBCCD, while we deliver the high-quality Microsoft and professional development training your team needs.

Qualifying for ETP Funding

California companies may qualify if they:

  • Are located in California and employ California residents
  • Provide full-time employment opportunities
  • Commit to retaining trainees for a specified period post-training
  • Contribute to California's unemployment insurance fund
  • Meet wage and job creation requirements
